The Underworld is the surface layer of the Abyss and is where unrestrained souls in the Material Plane gravitate towards. Because of this, deities aligned with death and management of souls make this their primary domain. Myrkul, Kelemvor, Naralis, Vandria, and to a lesser extent Shar often claim or reside here. Following the Calamity, Kelemvor imprisoned Myrkul and instituted a control and monitoring system of souls that arrive in the Underworld. Because of this change, souls fall into the lower layers of the Abyss much less frequently. Souls are also more likely to reincarnate in Kelemvor's system, leading to a higher prevalence of innate magical ability in living beings.
