Mortal civilizations on Erdniss overwhelmingly use a 12-month calendar year similar to earth. A year on Erdniss is 360 days, with each month lasting 30 days. The following list compares each Erdniss month to its real life counterpart.
1. Saluary - January
2. Hanali - February
3. Tyril - March
4. Ardri - April
5. Silvaber - May
6. Sahune - June
7. Aumber - July
8. Gruli	- August
9. Chaunery - September
10. Shaber - October
11. Magober - November
12. Kelember - December
