Flora and fauna in Kalypto has large inspirations from ancient life on earth, Final Fantasy, and other general fantasy tropes. When determining the flora and fauna in a region, environmental niches should be examined and filled with appropriate life. They can be generalist and specialist in nature, which means they are suited for many environments or specific ecosystems respectively. The abundance of energy in Kalypto also allows for magical variation in flora and fauna, although magic often comes with high exhaustion or nutritional requirements. Most magical beings are specialists in their ecosystem. One exception is dragons, who have magical and physical variations like specialists but are able to adapt quickly to new ecosystems like generalists.
