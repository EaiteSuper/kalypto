Jergal is an ancient mage from the [[2 Divinity Era]] that, through a failed magical ritual to guarantee eternal life for himself, opened such a vulnerability in his soul that he is at the whims of divine influence. However, this also turned him into the strongest form of cleric, an Exarch. His ritual side effects include sensitivity to soul movements and transfers, leading him to be a valuable asset to deities interested in controlling souls, monitoring deaths and new life, and tracking other deities. Because of this, Jergal has become the scribe of death for dominant death gods, including Myrkul, Kelemvor, and Hiatea.
