The Tenets of Fanteru is commonly seen in the Kingdom of Fanteruheim. The gods worshiped are Gond and Tharmekhûl. 

Gond is the forge god of innovativeness, and he represents Fanteru society’s strive for technological progress. 

Tharmekhûl is the forge god of the hearth, and he represents the resilience of the Fanteru against the cold, mountainous environment that they reside in.
