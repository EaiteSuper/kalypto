Beginning in the dry highlands of South Doriare in the century following the implementation of the Divine Barrier, Adjudico later became the state religion of the Holy Empire of Saphijerva. The main god worshiped is Amaunator, the sun god. 

Adjudico prioritizes honesty and lawfulness and disparages shady actions and perceived nontraditions like homosexuality. The religion uses light as its justification for inquisitions (bring things “to light”, banish shadows).

Other deities associated with light and radiance are respected, however they are not idolized as much as Amaunator. There is no specific evil entity like in Uftan. Evil is typically represented by known evil gods and even just gods that are linked to the concept of darkness, like death gods.
